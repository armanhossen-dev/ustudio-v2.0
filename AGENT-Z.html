AGENT Z
MISSION STRINGSHOT

Tahmid Al Zahin’s world is turned upside down by a series of tragic losses. Returning to Bangladesh, he’s unexpectedly drawn into the elite Hit Squad, training with his friend Nafiz to become world-class spies. Their mission: to stop the elusive Stringshot. Now, Tahmid must juggle his father’s business and his new life as a spy.
